What is TREC?

TREC's aim is to increase and support the research community focused on large-scale information evaluation and retrieval by providing a community of users an easy means of comparing their results under a common set of evaluation methods.

What do these P10, P20 and MAP numbers mean?
I thought this was about search results not geography why do we need maps?

P10 = The relevancy of the first 10 results displayed by the search engine.
P20 = The relevancy of the first 20 results displayed by the search engine.
MAP = MAP stands for Mean Average Precision which is the overall average.

Oh... so what does a track consist of?

Think of a track like a category of problem. There are a lot of different types of problem out there in the world. What's important enough about these problems for us to tell you are the title, genre and general description of the problem. Within each track you'll discover a whole bunch of fun tasks to compete against other researchers and solve. How many leaderboards will you rise to the top of?
